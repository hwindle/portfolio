Filters we Supply

    • Vintage cars
  • Commercial vehicles
    • Vans

    • Plant machinery
    • Cranes

AB Components Ltd is a filter distributor based in the East Midlands, between Derby and Nottingham. We have also acquired Talbot Components, based in Shropshire.

Talbot Components

For Talbot Components sales, email: sales@talbotcomponents.co.uk or phone on 01630 653 551. Talbot Components is based in the West Midlands, in Market Drayton. They supply filters, Millers Oils, bulbs, wiper blades and other consumables in Shropshire and the surrounding counties.

We aim to supply replacement filters with original equipment quality at competitive prices. We are supplied by the world's leading filtration manufacturers.
Our commitment to all customers, old and new, is to maintain the highest standards of service and quality.

We offer a local delivery service and a national and international parcel/pallet service.
