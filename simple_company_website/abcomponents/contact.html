Information Required for Quotes

  • Part Number

    When providing a part number, if possible, also supply filter type (air, oil, fuel, water, hydraulic) and Brand. It is helpful to have this additional information as not all part numbers are unique.

  • Application Details

    When supplying application details make, model and filter type required is usually enough to provide a quotation. However, serial number and year of manufacture are occasionally required as model specifications may change with time.
